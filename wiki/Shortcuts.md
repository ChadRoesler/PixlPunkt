## Customizing Shortcuts

1. Go to **Edit ? Preferences ? Keyboard** <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ChadRoesler/PixlPunkt/main/docs/assets/icons/keyboard_16.png" width="16">
2. Search for the action you want to customize
3. Click the shortcut field
4. Press your desired key combination
5. Click **Apply**

### Conflict Resolution
If your shortcut conflicts with an existing one, you'll see a <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ChadRoesler/PixlPunkt/main/docs/assets/icons/warning_16.png" width="16"> warning and can choose to:
- Replace the existing shortcut
- Cancel and choose a different key

### Reset to Defaults
Click **Reset All** in the Keyboard preferences to restore default shortcuts.
